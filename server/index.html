VARNAM 2022
----------
OUR AIM
Varnam'22 is focusing on developing and helping the students showcase their talents through various artistic forms and events conducted on the day. Students develop much unity and benevolence among teams and join together towards the successful festivity of Arts fest.
OUR SCOPE
Varnam 2022 aims at nurturing the biennial and artistic talents among students. Varnam organizes events at the inter-college level, providing on more opportunities and platforms for the students to nourish and sharpen their inner talents and taste in arts.

SPARK 2022
----------
OUR AIM
Spark 2022 is the sports festival conducted at intercollegiate level of MITS. It provides platform for students to give their best shot through various sport events organized.
OUR SCOPE
Spark'22 enables the students forward to unity, leadership and teamwork that maintains great solemnity, that helps the students focus on the physical and mental health contributing towards a successful event.
