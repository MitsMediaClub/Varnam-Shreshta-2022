+Rules and Regulations

1. Participation

1.1 The entire events of the sports meet will be held house wise.

1.2 All candidates who wish to participate in any events must submit their entries through house captains before the scheduled date.

1.3 Maximum 4 students can participate from each house for an event.

1.4 Each participant can take part in only 3 individual events and 2 group events.(Relay, Tug of war, pyramid will not be considered as group events)

2. March Past

2.1 It is mandatory for all houses to participate in opening function.

2.2 Participating teams must wear team uniform for the march past.

2.3 Participating Teams must bring house flag for March past.

3. Attire / Clothing

3.1 Only athletic uniform will be allowed for each competitor to participate in the athletic meet.

4. Reporting for events

4.1 Athletes must report for their event at least 30 minutes before the start of the event at call room.

5. Grievance

5.1 Grievance, if any, must lodge before the Grievance redressal committee before the announcement of the result.

5.2 The decision of the Grievance redressal committee will be final and binding.

6. Scoring System

6.1 Point awarded for individual events First - 5 Points Second - 3 Points Third - 1 Point

6.2 Point awarded for group events: First - 10 Points Second - 6 Points Third - 2 Points

6.3 For Overall Championship aggregate points from all events which include Men and Women will be considered.

6.4 For Overall Championship aggregate points from all events which include Men and Women will be considered. Best athlete in Men & Women will be selected as per the total individual points.

7. General Information

7.1 At competition arena Athletes should maintain strict discipline.

7.2 The sports committee reserves all right for the sports meet schedule and alteration may be done during exigencies for the smooth conduct of the events without prior notice.

7.3 The decision of the sports committee/officials will be final on all point of fact.

7.4 Athletes, Officials and Volunteers are only allowed to enter the competition arena.

8. Group Events

8.1 Badminton:- A group of 5 players each for boys and girls is required.The team will have 2 singles, 1 doubles pair and a substitute player. Same players cannot attend singles and doubles events.

8.2 Basketball:- A team of 10 players is required for basketball from which 5 players will play at a time.

8.3 Cricket:- A match of 6 overs will be played as knockouts and 8 overs for finals. Team of 15 members.

8.4 Football:-It will be played as 10s with a game length of 30 minutes each including extra time.The team should have 14 players including 4 substitute players. Extra time and penalty would be played only for the final match in case of a draw.

8.5 Volleyball:- is for boys and Throwball for girls. Both matches will be played on the same day. A team of 10 members for both.

8.6 Tug of war:-The maximum weight limit is 500 kg if exceed the team size must be reduced.

8.7 Aerobics:-A team of 15. This event is for girls only(From 1st and 2nd years only). A maximum time of 6 minutes is allocated for each house. The costume shall be a House jersey.

8.8 Pyramid:-A team of 7 members.
